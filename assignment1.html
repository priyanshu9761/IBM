Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein (14 March 1879 – 18 April 1955) was a German-born theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics.

Early Life

Einstein was born in Ulm, in the Kingdom of Württemberg in the German Empire on 14 March 1879.

Theory of Relativity

Einstein is best known for developing the theory of relativity, but he also made important contributions to quantum mechanics and statistical mechanics.

Legacy

Einstein is regarded as one of the greatest physicists of all time. His work is known for its influence on the philosophy of science.
